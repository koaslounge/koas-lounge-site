Band Booking

Want to play at Koa's Lounge?

Submit your band information, share links that let us hear your sound, and we’ll review whether the fit is right for an upcoming night at Koa’s.

Before you submit

Give us enough to hear the vibe and evaluate the fit.

Please include your band name, direct contact details, and links to the platforms where we can hear or watch you perform. The more complete your submission is, the easier it is for us to review quickly.

  • Band name, contact email, and phone are required
  • At least one performance or social link is required
  • You can include Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, Spotify, website, or another useful link
This form is configured for Netlify Forms, so once the site is deployed on Netlify, submissions can be collected directly from your dashboard.
