Our Story

Built with purpose, passion, and aloha.

Koa’s Lounge is a labor of love brought to life by husband-and-wife duo Christopher and Shaun Sibel. Their story began in Hilo, where they first met and shared a mutual love for the vibrant island culture, and blossomed into marriage in the picturesque town of Hawi.

With a shared passion for hospitality and community, they have dedicated their lives to creating unique experiences that celebrate the spirit of Hawaii. As the owners of Koa's Events and Koa's Mobile Bar, they bring their commitment to excellence and island-inspired hospitality into everything they do.

They envisioned Koa’s Lounge as a place that feels like an extension of home—a welcoming, creative space where friends and family can come together to enjoy great food, drinks, and unforgettable moments.

Their love for the Big Island shines through in every detail, from the bohemian decor to the handcrafted, locally inspired offerings. Their journey together is a testament to their dedication to each other, their community, and the island they call home.

Mission

More than a bar. A place to belong.

At Koa’s Lounge, our mission is to celebrate the spirit of Hawaii by creating an inclusive space where people can come together, enjoy exceptional food and drinks, and feel at home.

We are committed to supporting local artists and ingredients while crafting a genuine experience for our community.

Our goal is to be a place where meaningful moments and connections are made, reflecting the vibrant flavors and relaxed lifestyle of the islands.
