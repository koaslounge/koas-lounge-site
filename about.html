Welcome

Welcome to Koa’s Lounge, a vibrant bohemian-style cocktail lounge in the heart of Pāhoa, Hawaiʻi. Here, the laid-back rhythm of the island meets a room designed for flavor, music, and atmosphere.

Koa’s Lounge was created to feel expressive, warm, and memorable — a place for date nights, celebrations, casual meetups, and spontaneous evenings that turn into stories worth retelling.

What Makes It Different

The experience is built around more than just drinks. It is about the whole night: the lighting, the sound, the crowd, the cocktails, and the feeling that something is happening.

Whether you come for a DJ set, a live band, karaoke, or a quiet first round that turns into a long evening, Koa’s Lounge is designed to keep the night moving in the right direction.

Our Story

Christopher and Shaun Sibel built Koa’s Lounge as a labor of love.

Their story began in Hawaiʻi and grew into a shared life built around creativity, hospitality, and bringing people together. That same spirit now lives inside Koa’s Lounge — a place designed to feel stylish, social, and deeply connected to the island.

As the owners of Koa’s Events and Koa’s Mobile Bar, Chris and Shaun have spent years creating spaces where people can celebrate, connect, and make lasting memories. Koa’s Lounge became the next expression of that vision: a room where music, cocktails, conversation, and atmosphere all meet.

Every detail reflects that intention — from the bohemian design language to the warm energy of the space. The goal has never been to create just another bar, but a place people want to return to because of how it feels.
