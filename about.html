About Koa's Lounge

Island nights, crafted with warmth, music, and aloha.

Koa's Lounge brings together cocktail culture, live energy, and the creative soul of Pāhoa in a setting that feels intimate, expressive, and unmistakably Big Island.

Welcome

A lounge built for connection, flavor, and atmosphere.

Welcome to Koa’s Lounge, a vibrant, bohemian-style bar nestled in the heart of Pāhoa, Hawaii. At Koa’s Lounge, we blend the laid-back spirit of Hawaii with an eclectic, warm atmosphere that invites everyone to unwind and enjoy life's simple pleasures.

Inspired by the island’s unique culture and flavors, Koa’s Lounge is more than just a bar — it is a gathering place where locals and visitors alike can savor handcrafted cocktails, curated bites, live music, and nights that linger long after the last song.

Founded on the idea that a night out should be as much about connection as it is about cuisine and cocktails, Koa’s Lounge offers a welcoming space for date nights, celebrations, casual meetups, and spontaneous evenings that turn into memories.

Our story

Christopher and Shaun Sibel created Koa’s Lounge as a labor of love.

Their story began in Hilo, where they first met and discovered a shared love for Hawaii’s culture, creativity, and community. That connection eventually grew into marriage in Hawi — and later into a family of hospitality brands built around memorable experiences.

As the owners of Koa’s Events, a wedding and event venue in Mountain View, and Koa’s Mobile Bar, a traveling bar service serving the Big Island, Chris and Shaun have built their lives around bringing people together. Koa’s Lounge became the next expression of that vision: a welcoming, stylish place where great drinks, music, and conversation all meet.

Every detail reflects that intention — from the bohemian design language to the island-inspired energy of the room. Their hope is that Koa’s Lounge feels like an extension of home, but with the spark of a night out worth remembering.

“More than a bar, Koa’s Lounge is a place where meaningful moments happen, where people connect, and where the aloha spirit is felt in every detail.”

Mission statement
Mission

To create an inclusive place where great nights and genuine connections come naturally.

At Koa’s Lounge, our mission is to celebrate the spirit of Hawaii by creating a space where people can come together, savor exceptional food and drinks, and feel at home. We are committed to supporting local artists and ingredients while crafting a genuine experience for our community.

Our goal is to be more than just a bar. We want Koa’s Lounge to be a place where strangers become regulars, where local talent is celebrated, and where the relaxed, vibrant lifestyle of the islands comes through in every pour, every playlist, and every gathering.
