About Koa's Lounge

Island nights, crafted with warmth, music, and aloha.

Koa’s Lounge brings together cocktail culture, live energy, and the creative soul of Pāhoa in a setting that feels intimate and unforgettable.

What Defines the Space

Craft Cocktails Built for flavor, balance, and repeat orders
Live Music A rotating lineup that keeps every night fresh
Atmosphere Designed to pull you in and keep you there

More than a bar, Koa’s Lounge is a place where meaningful moments happen.

Our Story

Koa’s Lounge was built by Christopher and Shaun Sibel as a space where people can connect, celebrate, and enjoy the energy of the island.

From cocktails to music to atmosphere, everything here is intentional — designed to create a place people want to return to again and again.
